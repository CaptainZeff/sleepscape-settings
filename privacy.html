Sleepscape Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Sleepscape ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Alexa skill.

Information We Collect

Sleepscape may collect the following information:

  • Voice Commands: We process your voice commands to provide sleep sounds, stories, and other features. Voice recordings are handled by Amazon Alexa and are subject to Amazon's privacy policy.
  • User Preferences: We store your preferences (favorite sounds, voice settings, sleep timer preferences) to personalize your experience.
  • Subscription Information: If you subscribe to Sleepscape Premium, Amazon handles all payment processing. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status.
  • API Keys: If you provide a personal Gemini API key for AI features, it is stored securely and used only for your personalized experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our sleep sounds and stories
  • To remember your preferences across sessions
  • To generate personalized AI content when you use premium features
  • To manage your subscription status

Data Storage and Security

Your preferences are stored securely using Amazon DynamoDB. API keys are encrypted before storage. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • Amazon Alexa: Voice processing and skill hosting
  • Google Gemini: AI-powered personalized stories (if enabled)
  • Amazon Pay: Subscription payment processing

Children's Privacy

Sleepscape is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Disable the skill at any time through the Alexa app
  • Request deletion of your stored preferences
  • Cancel your subscription at any time
  • Remove your API key by saying "Alexa, ask Sleepscape to delete my settings"

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
